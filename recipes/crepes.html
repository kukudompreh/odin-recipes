Crepes are very thin pancakes. They can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings

You can fill your crepes with anything!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the milk, water, eggs and salt.
  2. Gradually whish in the flour and butter.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both side
